Candle Packaging Design

I have chosen to use my font for candle packaging,
which focuses on the color green, particularly tea green,
and features a tea leaf illustration. Key considerations
for this design include font choice, a green color palette,
a detailed tea leaf illustration, prominent brand logo and
product information, eco-friendly packaging materials,
related imagery, appropriate finish and texture, and
cohesive design. This comprehensive approach aims to
make a strong visual impact, effectively conveying the
essence of my tea-inspired candles.

SodaCan Packaging Design

I chose the Gothic theme for my soda can packaging design to impart a sense of mystery, elegance, and distinction to the product. The dark and intricate aesthetic of Gothic design stands out in a market saturated with conventional and vibrant packaging, instantly capturing the consumer's attention. This thematic choice not only creates a unique visual identity for the brand but also resonates with a niche audience appreciative of the unconventional and the emotionally evocative. By infusing the soda can design with Gothic elements, I aim to go beyond the ordinary, offering consumers an immersive experience.

Clothing Tag Design

I opted for a neoclassical theme in my clothing brand tag design to infuse a timeless and sophisticated elegance into the brand's identity. The neoclassical aesthetic, with its emphasis on symmetry, simplicity, and classical motifs, conveys a sense of enduring beauty and refined taste. By incorporating these elements into the tag design, I aim to evoke a feeling of luxury and craftsmanship, aligning the brand with a sense of tradition and enduring style.
